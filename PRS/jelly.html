Construction Jelly is a versatile and durable material used in various construction applications.

Composition

Construction Jelly is typically composed of a mixture of aggregates, binders, and additives to enhance its properties.

Properties

Construction Jelly exhibits excellent adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance, making it ideal for filling gaps, sealing joints, and as an adhesive.

Jelly Sizes

6mm x 12mm x 20mm x 40mm.

Applications

Used for sealing cracks, joints, and gaps in concrete, masonry, and other construction materials. It can also be used as an adhesive for bonding various construction elements.
