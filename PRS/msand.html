M-Sand Product Specifications

Product Features

M-Sand or manufactured sand, is a substitute for natural river sand used in construction.

Advantages

1. Environmentally friendly

2. Better concrete strength

3. Cost-effective

Applications

Used in concrete production, plastering, and masonry work.
